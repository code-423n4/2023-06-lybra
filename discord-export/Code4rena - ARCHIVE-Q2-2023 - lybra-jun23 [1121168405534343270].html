In the Lybra V2 protocol, we support using both rebase and non-rebase Lst as collateral, with each collateral type having its own vault. For non-rebase assets, since their collateral amount does not change, the protocol does not directly capture LSD earnings. The vault associated with this type of asset can mint peUSD (peg-eUSD) instead of eUSD. Therefore, the vault of this type can mint eUSD, allowing borrowers to enjoy stablecoin interest rates of up to 8%+.
